# Variables

## Events 最新消息

* Events[i].EventName (主旨)
* Events[i].CreateDate (建立日期)
* Events[i].Content (消息內容)
* Events[i].Creator (消息建立者)
* Events[i].EventID
* Events[i].Priority
* Events[i].PriorityUntil

## Projects 專題

* Projects[i].LabName (實驗室名稱)
* Projects[i].Description (實驗室介紹)
* Projects[i].ProjectID

## Albums 相簿

* Albums[i].AlbumName (相簿名稱)
* Albums[i].URL (相簿連結)
* Albums[i].CoverImg (相簿的封面照片)
* Albums[i].CreateDate (相簿建立時間)
* Albums[i].Creator (相簿建立者)
* Albums[i].AlbumID
